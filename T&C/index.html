Please note: For every successful lease or rental transaction facilitated through our platform, there will be an additional charge applied to the payment amount. The charge percentages are as follows: 10% for payments of ₦200,000, 15% for payments of ₦500,000, and 20% for payments of ₦700,000. For example, if the rental or lease amount is ₦200,000, the total amount displayed will be ₦220,000; for ₦500,000, the total amount displayed will be ₦575,000; and for ₦700,000, the total amount displayed will be ₦840,000.

Please note: For homes or office spaces with rental or lease amounts exceeding ₦800,000, there will be an additional charge of 25% applied to the payment. This means that the total amount payable will be the original rental or lease amount plus 25% of that amount.
